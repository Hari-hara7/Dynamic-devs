About CSI Club

The CSI Club (Computer Society of India) is dedicated to fostering a deep understanding of computing technologies. Our members engage in various activities ranging from coding competitions to technology seminars. The club provides a platform for students to enhance their skills in computer science and engineering.

Whether you're a beginner in coding or an experienced developer, the CSI Club offers a space for everyone passionate about technology and computing.
