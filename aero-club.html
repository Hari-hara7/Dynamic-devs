About Aero Club

The Aero Club is dedicated to exploring the world of aeronautics and aviation. We provide a platform for students to learn about aircraft design, flight mechanics, and the latest developments in the aerospace industry.

Whether you are passionate about flying, engineering, or just curious about aeronautics, the Aero Club offers opportunities to dive deep into the field and turn your passion into practical skills.
